Our Mission

The mission of our NGO is to empower the mental health of child, adolescent, youth, elderly and families by providing enrichment activities and trainings.

Our Vision

The vision is to make a better and positive world for the families in Society. Especially a the community which is well equipped with mental health values and principles. Child and Youth friendly settings for better outcomes.
